Download & Install Update

Easy Install Steps

1
Tap Download Button
Tap the green button above to download the update.apk package.
2
If Chrome Warns, Tap "Download anyway"
Chrome flags all apps outside the Play Store. Tap "Download anyway" to proceed.
3
Allow Source in Settings (One-Time Setup)
If Android says "not allowed to install unknown apps from this source", tap "Settings" in that popup, turn on "Allow from this source", and go back.
4
Tap Install & Launch
Tap "Install" or "Update", and your app will launch with the new features!
